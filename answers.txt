Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give and Example and how it might help

Answer: Data science is making a real difference in cancer research,
helping scientists sift through huge amounts of medical and biological 
data to spot trends, make predictions, and even discover new treatment options.
For example, machine learning is being used to predict how patients might 
respond to treatments like chemotherapy or immunotherapy, helping doctors make more 
informed decisions.
